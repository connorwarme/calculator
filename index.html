KAUKULATOR

Inspired by Ron Kauk, a climbing pioneer and legend.

  • First Ascents include the iconic lines Astroman (5.11c), Midnight Lightning (V8), and Magic Line (5.14c)
  • "Take care of nature, and nature will take care of you."
  • Founded Sacred Rok, a nonprofit seeking to "support youth in nature, helping youth learn to respect nature and through that, to respect themselves."
